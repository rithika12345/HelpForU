about us

Corona virus is a global crisis and the biggest challenge we have faced in our life time
In a Crisis, there are many unknown variables and hence the system always fights it with a reactive approach. We go from handling one crisis to another. It is a sort of fire fighting. Each person is struggling and fighting in their own way.
There was a case in Assam where a doctor self-medicated himself with anti-malarial drug HCQ tablet as prophylaxis as he was handling Covid-19 patients and then he died due to cardiac arrest. There are people who need help so there is a need for ideas not for curing the pandemic but to help people by developing small but important and essential daily needs during this pandemic.
On this motive we came up with idea to develop a COVID-19 HELP DESK which helps people to reach hospital on time and save lives.

Problems FacedThe problems are described below

After searching about problems related to our topic, these are the problems that we have identified which are repeatedly common :
Lack of beds in hospital: As pandemic intensifies, many in India are dying due to shortage of hospital beds.
Shortage of time: Searching a proper hospital manually takes a lot of time. The person may even die while roaming from one hospital to another.
Ambulance problem: Sometimes ambulance takes a lot of time to reach the patient’s house. Also there is a shortage of ambulance in almost every city.
Population of covid patients in hospitals: In this pandemic time, people with other health ailments are also facing issues as they are afraid to go to hospitals where the population of covid patients is really huge.

Objective of our WebsiteThe objectives are described below

We are thinking of tackling these problems by designing a website. The problems that we have mentioned above and their respective solutions are:
1.Sometimes some hospitals get completely filled while some remains empty but due to lack of proper information and management people face problems in finding such empty hospitals. To solve this problem we will mention the name of the hospital and the bed availability in that particular hospital every hour.
2.Once a person finds a proper hospital with bed, a lot of time will be saved and the person will get the treatment in proper time without unnecessarily roaming around.
3.At this time many people find it hard to get an ambulance, So we are enabling a ambulance tracker system in our website .

4.We will also mention the number of covid patients admitted in a particular hospital in our website. Through this people suffering from other health ailments can have prior information about it and can decide the hospital accordingly keeping in mind about the risk of getting infected from the virus.
5.We will also mention the availability of the ventilators in a hospital so that the critical patients can be admitted on time.
6.We will also take feedbacks from the patients after they get cured and post it in the website so that people can make their decisions before admitting themselves in a hospital.
7.We will also add a booking system so that the patient can book their seats in prior so that in reaching the hospital the treatment will start as soon as possible.
